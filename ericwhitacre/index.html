Please join us to watch this unique Facebook Live event hosted by Eric Whitacre featuring three different choirs performing live four hundred miles apart within the state of California. This online performance will utilize JackTrip Virtual Studio to connect three choral groups to perform Eric’s new musical composition, “Sing Gently” for upper voices.

The event includes over 400 miles of separation between the Northern and Southern choir groups! With over 80 singers involved at this event, each one is to perform live from their home utilizing the JackTrip Virtual Studio
